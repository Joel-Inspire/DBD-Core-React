Customer Quote Output

 

DB Distributor > Request for Quotation System > Processing > Customer Quote Output (RFQ A3)

 

The Customer Quote Output is used to send your customer a quote after you have received your cost quotes back from your vendor(s), and decided upon your sell price.

 

Follow these steps to print the quote:

  1. The options field will normally be set to a "1". This is for the standard format of the Customer Quote.
  2. You can enter in a range or RFQ numbers to control which customer quotes will be sent out.
  3. Even if you will be faxing or e-mailing the quotes, leave the "output" field set to "printed requests".
  4. The "user defined spec format" field can be left blank, as it is not used with electronic forms.
  5. Your Output Options button should be defaulted to N0.
  6. Press the Print Report button to print/fax/e-mail your quotes to the customers.
