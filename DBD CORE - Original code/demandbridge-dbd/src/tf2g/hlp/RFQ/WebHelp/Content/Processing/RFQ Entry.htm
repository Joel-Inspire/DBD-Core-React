RFQ Entry

 

DB Distributor > Request for Quotation System > Processing > RFQ Entry (RFQ A1)

 

The RFQ Entry screen is used for setting up your quotes to go out to your vendors, entering in quoted costs from your vendors, and determining your sell price to quote to the customer/prospect.

 

The RFQ Entry panel is divided into 3 sections. The header area, the line area, and the vendor area.

 

Follow these steps to complete the panel:

  1. Press the Add New RFQ button to automatically assign a new quote number. You also have the option to use the lookup button to find open RFQ's, or type in an existing RFQ number to edit/view that quote.
  2. For a new quote, enter in the salesperson that is responsible for this quote. You will then need to fill out the header area.

 

Note: Pressing the Save button will save the record and clear out the screen. Pressing the Next button will take you to the lines area.
