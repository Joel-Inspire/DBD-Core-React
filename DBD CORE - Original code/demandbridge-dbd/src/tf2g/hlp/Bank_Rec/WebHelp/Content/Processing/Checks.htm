Bank Statement Data - Checks

 

DB Distributor > Bank Reconciliation > Processing > Bank Statement Data > Checks (B/R P1 [F5])

 

This is the area of the bank reconciliation where you will come to designate your checks as being "cleared" or "outstanding".

 

Follow these steps to complete the panel:

 

  1. Enter in a check number to clear, or use the lookup to see a listing of checks that have been processed by the system.
  2. On the right side of the screen, you can designate if the check has been cleared, or if there is a discrepancy. When you click on "Yes" or "No" for the cleared field, the next check record will automatically be brought up. If you marked the check as a "discrepancy", proceed to the next step.
  3. If you marked the check as having a discrepancy in the previous step, a field will appear where you can enter in the amount that the bank paid out for the check. There is also a field where you will enter in if the error was a bank error. The way that you answer this question will determine if the difference of funds will show in the G/L Adjustments field, or the Plus/Minus Bank Errors field. Note: GL Adjustments will post to the account you specified in step 7 of your Bank Account setup.
  4. You also have the option of clearing a range of checks. If you click the Range button, you will be able to enter in starting and ending check numbers, and have the entire range marked as cleared.
  5. After matching all of your checks from the statement, press the Exit button.
