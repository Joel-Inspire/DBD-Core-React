PM Customer - Reporting Controls

 

Field Description
Customer Group Code If this customer is to be part of a customer group, enter a customer group code.
Should Dist Owned Inventory be reported Y or N. This determines if distributor owned inventory is reported as part of the on hand amount for PM reports. DB Distributor recognizes the issues that distributors face when deciding whether or not to report form availability. In some situations, a distributor may not want to show large on-hand quantities that resulted from a quantity buy as this may give some customers the feeling that order turnaround will not be an issue with such adequate supplies. Other distributors may face customers that may feel inhibited in ordering a form if large quantities do not seem to be available on an on-going basis. This parameter controls whether or not form quantities from distributor owned inventory should be added to customer owned inventories on PM reports.
Does customer use FMS% Pricing If the customer charges their using locations a different price than you charge to them, set this to "Y".
FMS Markup Entered as a percentage. This would be a standard FMS markup for your customer to charge their locations.
Standard Inventory Valuation Method Is the inventory value based on sell price, FMS sell price, or a bundled sell price?
Reduce On-hand by Committed on Reports If set to "Y", the on-hand amount will be reduced by any committed quantity on the reports listed.
