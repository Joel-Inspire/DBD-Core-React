How to Enter a Credit Memo

 

This process is for issuing a complete credit to your customer. You will want to have the File Copy of the invoice that was originally issued, or a copy of the Detail Sales Journal. Once you enter into AR Invoice Entry, complete the following steps:

 

  1. In the header area, you will need to assign a new invoice number. The order number will be left blank.
  2. Enter in the customer code, and ship-to, if needed, that the credit should be entered for.
  3. In the "type" field, you can designate that this invoice is a "credit memo". Doing so will bring up an additional panel where you can list the original order number and invoice that the credit is being issued for. You can also list notes as to why the credit is being issued. This information is written to a file for ODBC access.
  4. In the lines area, you will need to add all of the lines exactly as they appear on the Detail Sales Journal, or from the File Copy of the original invoice. The only difference is that you will enter a negative quantity ordered on each line.
  5. Continue to the summary area to verify your invoice total. It should be a negative amount that is equal to the amount of the original invoice.
  6. You will then need to update the invoice like normal to have your credit created. To apply the credit invoice to your original invoice in Cash Receipts, you can follow these instructions.

 

Note: If this is a credit where the goods are going back to the original vendor, and you are expecting to receive a credit invoice from the vendor, you can use an alternate method. This would involve creating a new order, that matched the original order, with the exception of having negative quantities ordered. You can then take this "credit" order through the PO Receiving and Invoicing process to generate your credit AP invoice, and your credit invoice to the customer.
