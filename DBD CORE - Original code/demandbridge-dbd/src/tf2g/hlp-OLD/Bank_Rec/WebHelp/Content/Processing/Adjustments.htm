Bank Statement Data - Adjustments

 

DB Distributor > Bank Reconciliation > Processing > Bank Statement Data > Checks (B/R P1 [F7])

 

The Bank Rec module allows you to enter in GL adjustments as needed. This is where you will come to enter in any adjustments.

 

Follow these steps to complete the panel:

 

  1. Enter in an adjustment number to use, or press the Assign Next button to let the system assign the next adjustment number.
  2. Enter the adjustment date. This should be within the month that you are reconciling.
  3. Enter in the description/reason for the adjustment.
  4. Enter in the amount of the adjustment. Make sure to use the "minus" sign if needed.
  5. Enter in the general ledger account this adjustment should post to. Note: The balancing post will be made to the GL account setup in step 7 of the Bank Account setup.

 

Note: As an alternative to entering the adjustments here, you can also make Journal Entries through the General Ledger system, update the Detail Transaction Register, and then return here to see the updated balances.
