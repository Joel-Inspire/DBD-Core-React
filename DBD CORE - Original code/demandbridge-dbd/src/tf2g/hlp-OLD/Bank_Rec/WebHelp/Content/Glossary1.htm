Glossary of Terms

 

The following is a list of terms that you will want to be familiar with while working in the Bank Reconciliation module:

 

Adjustment This will include service charges by the bank, as well as bank corrections for errors.
Bank Error A discrepancy which was made by the bank (wrong amount paid or deposited). This can be designated by the user during the reconciliation.
Cleared An item that been processed through DB, and processed through the bank.
Discrepancy A difference between the posted amount in the Bank Rec system and the amount cleared by the bank.
Future GL A check or deposit which is paid on the bank statement, but was updated on the books in a future fiscal year and accounting period.
NOF Not on file. Typically a check that has been paid by the bank, but has not been entered into DB Distributor. Could also be a deposit on record with the bank, but not in DB.
Outstanding

An item which has been entered into DB, but has not yet cleared the bank.
Reconciliation The process of comparing and matching figures from the accounting records against those shown on a bank statement.
