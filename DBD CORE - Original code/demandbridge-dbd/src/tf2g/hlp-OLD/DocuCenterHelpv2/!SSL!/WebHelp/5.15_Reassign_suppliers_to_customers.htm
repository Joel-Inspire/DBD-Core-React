5.15 Reassign suppliers to customers

A DocuCenter supplier is not associated with any company by default. After you assign a supplier to a customer, the catalogs belonging to that company are visible and accessible to the supplier. The supplier will also be available for selection in the customer's shipping address information page.

 

You may associate a supplier with more than one company. This may be done by selecting a supplier and assigning multiple companies to this user. A Company may also be selected and associated with more than one supplier. This allows many suppliers to access and download a company's catalog items.

Note: Permission may be applied to Administrators only.
