TopForm Variable Print Option for Web.ec

The TopForm Variable Print option is a powerful tool to enable your customers to easily purchase customized variable text items via TopForm Web.ec on your web site. The most common example of a variable print item is the typical business card. However you can also offer your customers the ability to order and proof the following:

  • Stationery, envelopes, memo pads and Rolodex cards

  • Imprinted Commercial Print Items

  • Teller specific Forms

  • Imprinted Ad Specialty Items

  • Imprinted On-Demand Printing

Your customers enter and proof their own orders for these items from the TopForm Web.ec portion of your web site, and orders automatically flow through your TopForm order entry and accounting system. With the TopForm Variable Print option, you are providing your customers with added convenience and a valuable service. You are also introducing a high degree of efficiency and quality into your operations. For more information, see the online Help or user guide on the Variable Print option.
