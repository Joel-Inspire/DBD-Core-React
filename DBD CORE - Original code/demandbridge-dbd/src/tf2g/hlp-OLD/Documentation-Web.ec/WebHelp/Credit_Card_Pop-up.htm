TopForm Credit Card Option for Web.ec

The TopForm Credit Card option enables your TopForm Web.ec customers to charge orders to their credit card (Visa, MasterCard, or American Express) on your web site. Once you have completed the Credit Card option setup, credit-card orders automatically flow through your TopForm order entry and accounting system based on the parameters you select for processing credit-card orders in TopForm. For more information, see the online Help or user guide on the Credit Card option.
