Introducing TopForm Web.ec

TopForm Web.ec is the integrated, web-based interface to TopForm systems. Distributors who purchase Web.ec can have their customers buy any product in their TopForm database directly from the their web site. Specifically, Web.ec provides distributors with the following capabilities and benefits:

  • Ability to publish your TopForm product catalog on your web site for secure online purchases by your customers' approved buyers, categorized according to each customer's desired specifications

  • Automated inventory and item management reporting through TopForm

  • Ability for customers to buy United Stationers office supply products on your web site. Note: Available only to distributors who purchase the Web.ec TOPS module.

  • Ability for customers to proof and order variable print items such as business cards, envelopes, and customized commercial print items on your web site. Note: Available only to distributors who purchase the Web.ec Variable Print option.

  • Ability for customers pay online using their credit card. Note: Available only to distributors who purchase the Web.ec Credit Card option.
