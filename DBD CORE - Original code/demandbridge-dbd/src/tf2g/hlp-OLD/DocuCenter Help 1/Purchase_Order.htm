Purchase Order

Queue, Lead Time and PO Activation

Purchase orders may be placed for items months in advance. These orders are held in a queue by DocuCenter and are not released to your manufacturer partners until a few days before the material is due to be produced and shipped. This allows your customers to make revisions to items until the last minute even if there are orders placed for them in the system. This is all totally transparent to your customers and they are free to revise their items to their heart’s content.

 

The administrator with appropriate permissions can set the lead time (in hours) which controls the number of hours by which a purchase order is released by the Manufacturer in advance so as to give them the time to produce and deliver it.

 

When a purchase order is activated, it is sent to the Manufacturer ready for download. An email notification is also sent out to all the Manufacturer contacts.

PO Download

  • The Manufacturer searches for purchase orders either by their delivery dates or by their download status. In the Downloadable Orders view, only purchase orders which have not yet been downloaded appear. Individual purchase orders are downloaded in the form of a compressed file with a .zip extension. Any commercial compression utilities like WinZip or StuffIt can be used to extract the contents of the .zip file.

File Layouts

A downloaded .zip file for an order contains the following:

  • Purchase order information data (Excel spreadsheet)
  • Item information for each item ordered (Excel spreadsheet)
  • Separate folder containing all artwork for each item ordered (Folder containing files)
