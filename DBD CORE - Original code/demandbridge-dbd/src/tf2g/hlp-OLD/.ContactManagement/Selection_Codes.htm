Selection Codes

Unlike the other codes discussed so far, Selection Codes are unique in that you can assign up to eleven selection codes per prospect.   Use selection codes as additional “hooks” for filtering through your contact database.  

 

Selection Codes to track Anniversaries - You might assign a selection code that identifies the anniversary of the month in which you started doing business with a specific customer.   Use the Profile feature to automatically create letters to thank your customer for their business around the time of their anniversary.

 

 

Selection Codes to track Collections – You may also want to use the Contact Management system to track your collection efforts.  It provides for

 

  • Immediate access to your customers real time receivables data

  • Follow up dates and follow up actions

  • Through TopForm WordLink   you will be able to have standard collection letter templates to confirm agreements and/or promises made with regard to invoice payments.  

 

 

Selection Codes to track quality control  - You may assign selections codes to specify certain actions or conditions with regard to quality control.  As you can have multiple sections codes for each contact, one could be used to specify the type of complaint while another cross-referenced the vendor involved.

 

 

Selections code to track vendor performance – If you use the Contact Management System to track your vendor relationships, you can use Selections codes to provide a central database that contains that types of products each vendor offers as well the vendor performance ratings.

 

 

When setting up Selections Codes, you can restrict their use to certain List Types to which they are applicable.     For instance, those selection codes that only related to Vendors, might be restricted to the VEN list, while those codes that are used only for collections might be restricted to the COL list.  

 

Highlight a List Type and use the  key to specify this List Type for the Selection Code.
