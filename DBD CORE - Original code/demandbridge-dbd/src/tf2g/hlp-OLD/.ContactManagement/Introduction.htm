TopForm® Contact Management

 

Introduction

 

 

The TopForm Contact Management module provides you with a vehicle to track notes and correspondence with your prospects and customers.  It also integrates closely with your accounts receivable account master so that order history and product detail will be close at hand.  As a result, your TopForm Accounts Receivable module should be implemented prior to setup of the Contact Management system.

 

This documentation is divided into three main sections:

  • Getting Started

  • Operations

  • Utilities

 

Getting Started will outline the setup required before your Contact Management System can be used.  Operations will cover the day-to-day use of the contact Management System.
