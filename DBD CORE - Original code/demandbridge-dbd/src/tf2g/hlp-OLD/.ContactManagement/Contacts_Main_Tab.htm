Contacts Main Tab

 

Lookup – Note that the first twenty characters of the company name will default in the Lookup field.  This field is used for alphabetical searches.  If you are entering an individuals name as the company name, for instance, Dr. Richard M. Smith, MD, you should probably change this default to SMITH, DR RICHARD to conform to standard alphabetizing rules.

 

Contact person – Enter the full name of the contact person.  This will be used for letter writing.

 

Title – Enter the title for the contact person.

 

Greeting – Enter the portion of the contact name that should follow the salutation (Dear) in a typical letter.  This contact would be greeted as “Dear George:”

 

Phone, Fax and e-mail – Enter this additional contact information.
