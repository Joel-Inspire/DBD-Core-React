Status Tab

 

Selections Codes - On the Status tab, you can choose the Selection Codes from your list of available codes.  

 

Last Contact Data - You can also enter Last Contact Date and Code.  Based upon the setting in the Action Code Setup, the Comment, the Follow-up Date, Code and Comment will automatically display.    This information will also automatically update from the notes that are entered on the Notes tab.

 

Barometer - The Barometer is a setting from zero to nine that indicates the relative interest level of the prospect.  This field can also be used as a filter when performing a contact Profile.

 

Annual Sales - The Annual Sales field can be used the store the annual sales revenue of your prospect or could alternatively be used to store the potential annual sales revenue you might derive form this prospect.
