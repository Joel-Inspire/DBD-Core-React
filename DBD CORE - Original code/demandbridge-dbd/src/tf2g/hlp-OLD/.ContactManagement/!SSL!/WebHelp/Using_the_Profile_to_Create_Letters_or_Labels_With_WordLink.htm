Using the Profile to Create Letters or Labels With WordLink

After turning the profile “On,” click on the WordLink Tab, click the “Clear” button to clear the panel of any contact information that may be active.

 

Date – Accept the defaulted date or enter a new date.

 

Template – From the drop down box, chose the template for your letter or your mailing label or both.  For more detailed instructions on how to create a template, see the earlier section regarding WordLink and creating templates.

 

Subject – Enter an optional subject line.  This can be one of your mail merge fields, or it can just update as part of the notes of your mailing.

 

Click the Word button.  One letter and/or label for each of the contacts in your profile will be created.
