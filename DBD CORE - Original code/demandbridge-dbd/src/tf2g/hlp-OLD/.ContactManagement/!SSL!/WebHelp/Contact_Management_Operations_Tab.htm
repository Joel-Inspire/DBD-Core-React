Operations Tab

Now that you have completed the initial setup of your Contact Management System, you are ready for your daily operations.  Daily operations could consist of the following:

 

  • Setting up new Contacts and modifying existing contacts

  • Entering Notes

  • Creating Letters

  • Creating Profiles

  • Printing Reports

 

As part of your daily operations also includes setting up new codes and contacts.  Please review the previous section for documentation on setup.
