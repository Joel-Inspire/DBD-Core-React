How to Make a Journal Posting to a Prior Year

 

Sometimes after closing out your Fiscal Year, you might find that you have a need to make adjustment postings to the year that you have closed. Here are the steps that you can use to make adjustments to a closed fiscal year.

 

  1. Go into the Fiscal Year Setup screen.
  2. Select the year that needs to be re-opened.
  3. If your security access level is high enough, you will have the option to re-open the year. In GUI, there will be a button to press. In CHUI, you will be prompted to press the F8 key.
  4. Proceed to the General Journal Entry screen.
  5. Make your posting to the correct Fiscal Year and Accounting Period. Your operator code must have access to make postings to a closed period in order to make this posting.
  6. Update your posting.
  7. Run and update your Detail Transaction Register.
  8. The next time you run your Period End Processing, this posting will be processed first, the Fiscal Year will be re-closed, and the appropriate updated balances will be rolled forward.
