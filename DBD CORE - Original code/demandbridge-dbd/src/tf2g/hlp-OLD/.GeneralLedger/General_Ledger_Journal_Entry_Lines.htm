General Ledger Journal Entry Lines

 

 

You can click on any line to make it the active line.  

 

  1. You can then click then "Del Ln" button to delete the active line.  

  2. Or you can modify the general ledger account or the amount or

  3. You can click on the "Ins Ln" button to insert a new line above the line selected.  See the illustration below.

 

By clicking on a line, then clicking on  the Insert line button, you open a space to insert a new general ledger posting.

 

Note that the debit and the credit totals of the Distribution balance must equal to be able to exit from the general ledger lines entry portion of this program.  Once the entry is in balance, you have the option the run and update the General Ledger Journal.
