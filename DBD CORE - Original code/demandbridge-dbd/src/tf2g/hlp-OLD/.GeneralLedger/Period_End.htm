Period End Processing

 

When you choose Period End Processing, the current period and year will display.  Review this data and make certain that it displays what you expect.

 

                                      

 

Once you click on the Proceed button, you will be presenting with additional information.

 

This panel restates what has been identified as your preferences in you General Ledger Parameters.  In the above example, it states that you have elected to retain general ledger postings for twelve periods .  It also displays the period and fiscal year postings that will be removed during this period end update.  If this is not what you intended, click the “No” button and choose the general ledger parameter option from the Setup/System Folder and extend the length of time that general ledger postings should be retained.  

 

Remember, disk space is usually not a problem.   It is better to have more data than not enough.
