TopForm General Ledger Financial Statements

 

There are two basic financial reports that you can print from your TopForm General Ledger:

 

  • Balance Sheet

  • Profit and Loss or Income Statement

 

You also have several options when printing these reports.  With regard to the Balance Sheet, you can print a standard balance sheet, or you can print a prior year comparison.

 

With regard to the Profit and Loss Statement you have three options.

 

  • Month to Date and Year to Date

  • Prior Year Comparison

  • Budget Actual Comparison
