Period End Processing

TopForm is period driven, therefore, you do not have to perform Period End Processing before you begin making entries in the new period.  However, General Ledger Period End Processing should be performed as soon as possible following the completion of the following:

 

  1. All entries in your other TopForm modules have been completed for the period.  

  2. Your Bank Reconciliation for the period has been completed and all reconciliations of your other control accounts have been verified

  3. Adjusting and recurring journal entries have been updated.

  4. Financial Reports have been printed and/or archived.

 

Period End Processing performs the following actions in your TopForm Accounting System.  

 

  • Cycles the accounting period.

  • Based upon the general ledger parameters, period end checks to see if any    general ledger postings should be removed.

 

Year-End Processing

 

Following Period End Processing for the final period in your fiscal year,  the Year-End Processing programs will automatically be invoked.   Year-End Processing, performs the following tasks in your TopForm Accounting System:

 

  • Closes the current fiscal year and opens the new one.  

  • Transfers for each balance sheet account the ending balance for the current fiscal year to the beginning balance  the new fiscal year.

  • General Ledger Income and Expense accounts are combined, producing net profit or loss.  This number becomes the Retained Earnings Account's beginning balance for the new fiscal year.

  • For the company being closed, year-end processing retains general ledger postings and account balance summary records for as long as was specified in the General Ledger Parameters, and removes data exceeding your stated requirement.
