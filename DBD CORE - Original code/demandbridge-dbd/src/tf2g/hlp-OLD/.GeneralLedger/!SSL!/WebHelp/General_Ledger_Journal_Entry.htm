General Ledger Journal Entry

 

Choose the Journal Entry option from the  Journal Posting folder.

 

Fiscal Year and Accounting Period

 

 

One of the most important selections in an accounting entry is the fiscal year and accounting period.   This controls the period in which the posting will be made, regardless of the transaction date.  Note that the fiscal year and accounting period make up part of the key to the record.  Therefore, it cannot be changed. To correct an error, you must delete the entry and re-enter it from scratch, so pay attention to this very important component of every entry.

 

 

Enter the Source Journal.  You can use the query button to look up the available options. You should not choose any source journal code that belongs to another TopForm m module.  

                           

 

 

The only two appropriate choices from this list would be JE for Journal Entry and PR for Payroll Entry.  Since TopForm does not have a Payroll module you will probably use Journal Entries to record your payroll entries

Automatic Reversal Entries

 

 

 

TopForm provides you with the ability to automatically reverse certain entries.  As a part of your month end reconciliations, you might find that inventory has arrived in the warehouse, but the vendor invoice has not yet arrived.  You therefore have more inventory that your general ledger reflects and you have an obligation to a vendor that has not yet been entered into accounts payable.  To properly state inventory and liabilities, you should make the following entry:

 

Dr                     Cr

Inventory                                                     XXX

Accrued Payables                                                               XXX

 

However, next month, the vendor invoices will arrive.  You don’t want to have to treat these vendor invoices any differently than any of the others that are being entered.  However, if your operators post these vendor invoices to inventory, your inventory account will be overstated.  Therefore, you would set your journal entry to automatically reverse.  This allows you to properly state your financials at month end, but eliminates any special processing requirement on these entries.

 

To set an entry to automatically reverse, simply click on the “Reversal” arrow.  You will then be able to enter the year and accounting period to reverse this entry.  This will almost always be the period immediately following the fiscal year and accounting period of the original entry.

 

Description

 

 

You have three lines of description that you can attach to any journal entry.  When viewing or printing your general ledger report, you can elect to have the extending description display.

General Ledger Lines

 

Note:  Adding, deleting and modifying lines are common routines in all of the data entry programs involving general ledger postings.  Once you familiarize yourself with these routines, you will be proficient in the other programs as well.

 

You can click on any line to make it the active line.
