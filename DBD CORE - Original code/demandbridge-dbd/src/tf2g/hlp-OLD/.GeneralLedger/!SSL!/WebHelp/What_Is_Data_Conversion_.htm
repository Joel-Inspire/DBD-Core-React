What Is Data Conversion?

Data Conversion is the process of moving dat from your existing computer or manual system to the system new computerized system being implemented. Under most circumstances, at least part of this will be done through automated means. Proper planning is a prerequisite for a smooth transition.

 

The necessary steps for data conversion of your general ledger data are:

 

  • Setting up the Chart of Accounts

  • Entering the Account Balances

  • Verification of the Balances

 

Additionally, you may elect to:

 

  • Enter prior period activity for the current and preceding year. This allows you to take immediate advantage of comparative financial statements.

  • Enter budget amounts for prior and future periods in order to facilitate budget variance financial reporting.

 

Be sure to gather all your records before you begin the conversion process.

 

Read this entire section before you begin processing. If you are attempting the conversion process for the first time, refer to the Getting Started and the Daily and Periodic Processing  sections of this documentation to gain a thorough understanding of the operation of the General Ledger system.
