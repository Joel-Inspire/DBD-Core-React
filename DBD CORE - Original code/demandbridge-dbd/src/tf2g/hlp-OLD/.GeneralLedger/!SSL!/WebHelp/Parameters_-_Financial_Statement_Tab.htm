Parameters - Financial Statement Tab

 

Some of the headings within the Financial Statements can be modified to suit each company’s specific requirements.

 

Gross Profit

Enter the description for the Gross Profit line on the lncome Statement.

Net Income from Operations

Enter the description you want to use for Net Income from Operations on the Income Statement.

Net Profit

Enter the Income Statement description you wish to use for Net Profit

G/L Account For Net Profit

Enter the account number you plan to use for Net Profit on the Balance Sheet. This is normally found in the Equity or Capital section of your Chart of Accounts. Usually, this account number is not posted since the year to date Net Income on the Income Statement calculates its balance.

Liabilities & Capital

Enter the Balance Sheet description you wish to use for Liabilities & Capital. Liabilities and Owners Equity is another example.
