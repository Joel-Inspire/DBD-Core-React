Late In The Fiscal Year

If you are well into your accounting year, it is usually not advisable to go too far back in time for a conversion, unless this conversion can be done through automated means. Such an effort usually places great strains on the operator to “catch up", and leads to errors and frustration. If you are well into your fiscal year and your are converting manually,

 

  1. Begin your conversion as of the last day of the preceding closed period by entering the ending balances for each General Ledger Account using the Journal Entry Program. Follow the procedural steps outlined previously; except, under these circumstances, be sure to enter balances for your Income and Expense Accounts as well.

  2. Period End Processing in this case will just advance the period to the current accounting period.
