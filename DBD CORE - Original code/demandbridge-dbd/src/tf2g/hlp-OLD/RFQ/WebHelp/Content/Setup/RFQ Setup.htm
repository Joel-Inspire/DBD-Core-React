RFQ Setup

 

The setup of the RFQ module involves setting your parameters and creating vendor groups, if needed. In addition to creating RFQ's for your existing customers, you also have the option of entering quotes for prospective customers. These prospects will need to be setup within the RFQ module as well. You also have the option of entering in a quote for one of your contacts from the Contact Management module.

 

Note: While you do have the option of entering in quotes for "prospects" or other "contacts", only quotes for "customers" have the option of being turned into orders through the Order Processing module.
