How to Setup a Vendor to Enter Quotes Online

 

One of the most time consuming steps in the quoting process is collecting the quotes from the vendors, and getting them entered back into the system. Through the use of the eCommerce Toolkit, you can have the vendors enter in their quotes online.

 

Follow these steps to setup your vendors for online quote entry:

 

  1. Your vendor will need to be setup as a Customer. This is setup in Accounts Receivable > Setup > Customers. You can set them up using only the minimum required fields.
  2. You will then setup the eCommerce Customer Parameters for this newly created "customer". This is done in eCommerce > Setup > Customer Parameters.
  3. You will then need to create a Buyer record for them to sign online with. This is setup in eCommerce > Setup > Buyers. The buyer will need to be setup by putting their "customer" code in the customer field, and their vendor code in the vendor field.
  4. Optionally, you can enter in your CSR e-mails in the Data Transport option. This will cause your CSR listed on the quote to receive an e-mail when the vendor enters in their quote information online. This is setup in Setup > System Utility Functions > Other Setup > Transport Parameters. You will select the CSR RFQ e-mail type.
  5. The vendor will then have the ability to login through your eCommerce website. When they login using their Buyer ID, the quotes available to them will be displayed. They will have the ability to add costs to each quote, or they can choose to enter no bid on the jobs. After entering in their quotes an e-mail will be sent to the CSR on the quote letting them know that the vendor has submitted their bid. The quote will automatically be updated with the quoted costs for that vendor.
