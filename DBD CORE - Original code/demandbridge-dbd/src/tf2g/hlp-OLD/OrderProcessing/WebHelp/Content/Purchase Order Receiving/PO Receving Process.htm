Purchase Order Receiving Process

The Purchase Order Receiving Process allows you to receive purchase order information and inventory for Sales or Bill As Shipped orders previously entered in Order Processing. The PO Receiving and Updating process consists of the following areas:

 

  1. P/O Receiving Panel
  2. P/O Special Shipping Receiving Panel (special shipping only)
  3. Inventory Lot Receiving Panel (special shipping inventory only)
  4. Receiving Report and Update Panel
