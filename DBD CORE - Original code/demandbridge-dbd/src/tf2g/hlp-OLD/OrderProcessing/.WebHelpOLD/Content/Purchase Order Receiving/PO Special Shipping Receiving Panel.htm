P/O Special Shipping Receiving Panel

 

TopForm > Order Processing > Purchase Orders > Processing > Purchase Order Receiving > P/O Special Shipping Receiving (P/O P3)

 

Use the P/O Special Shipping Receiving Panel to adjust the Receive Now amount. Follow these steps to complete the panel:

 

  1. Confirm the information at the top of the panel.
  2. Optionally, modify the amount in the Receive Now field.
  3. Click Finish. The Inventory Lot Receiving Panel is displayed.
